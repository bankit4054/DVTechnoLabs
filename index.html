How we work

Our Project Process

  • Wireframing/Planning
  • Design
  • Development
  • Quality Assurance
  • Deployment

Before creating a product, a key understanding of the product is important. Wireframing is a process where we draw overviews of interactive products to establish the structure and flow of possible design solutions. These blueprints reflect user and business needs and help us come up with creative solutions for creating optimal prototypes and products by the following steps:

  • Collaboration with our customers at the beginning to develop a strong project plan.
  • Finalized scope as a launching point for the initial wireframes.
  • Placement of objects on each page of the application by the wireframing team.

After clear understanding of the requirements and creation of wireframes for all pages, It is important to follow the various stages involved in designing an interface that users will love and appreciate and are as follow:

  • Design is Understood and Reviewed by developers and clients.
  • Used by the design team to create the final look and feel.

After this stage you will be presented with your visionary prepossessing page with clickable versions. In this phase, the requirement included in the SRS document is used.

When the developer receives the Design document, implementation begins. The Software design is translated into source code.During this phase, all of the software components are implemented and make the application functional. In every step we keep our clients in loop and they can regularly monitor the development and can also provide feedback for their software

  • Developers select cutting edge and latest technologies to implement the design.
  • Expertise in smooth and light weight application and software database.
  • Designer, Developer and QA team works together to complete development within defined timeline.
  • Unit testing performed by developer to ensure expected results.

The developed software is thoroughly tested in this phase and any defects found are assigned to developers to be corrected. Regression testing is performed until the software meets the customer's expectations; testers then refer to the SRS document to make sure that the software meets the customer's standards. It’s advantages are:

  • Inspires Customer Confidence and Satisfaction.
  • Improves Security, Optimization, Efficiency.
  • Improves Product Quality and Performance.

Depending on customer expectations, a replica of the production environment is created and the developers perform the testing after it has been tested and deployed in the production environment, the application is released. After our internal QA, project management and the client have approved it, we are ready to deploy the live code to cloud or other environment if the customer finds the application as expected. After deployment of a product in a production environment,

  • Maintenance of the product is done.
  • Observation of performance and Security.
  • Constant touch with clients for any upcoming features.
